About

Because Book Publishing Should Be Built Around the Author

At Spines, our mission is to make professional publishing accessible to everyone, so no great story goes unheard. We combine expert care, smart technology, and a seamless experience to empower authors, amplify their voices, and honor the craft.

What book publishing should be

Designed for Authors Who Want More

Spines is a publishing platform created for authors who want more: More quality. More control. More reach. With Spines, you retain full ownership of your copyrights while accessing the tools and expertise to bring your vision to life with professionalism and precision.

Everything You Need to Bring Your Book to Life

Our platform combines expert support with purpose-built technology to deliver professional publishing at scale. Publish in print, ebook, and audiobook formats with custom design, professional editing, multi-language translation, and global distribution to over 100 channels.

Effortless Publishing with Expert Guidance

Intelligent workflows power every stage of the process, automating where it saves time and involving experts where it matters most. The result? A publishing experience thats faster, clearer, and built to meet the high standards you deserve.

Our culture and values

Books Are Our Legacy

We believe books are forever. They outlive us, outlast platforms, and carry meaning across generations. We publish stories that matter to preserve voices that might otherwise be lost. Our mission is to make professional publishing accessible to everyone, so no great story goes unheard.

Access for All

Publishing should be for everyone. Were opening the gates that traditional publishing closed, offering every writer the tools, support, and freedom to bring their story to life. No format, language, or budget should stand in the way.

Craft + Care

Details matterbecause your book matters. We combine artistic care with technological excellence to deliver books that authors are proud of and readers cherish. Quality isnt optional; its the standard.

Clarity

We tell it like it is. Publishing can be confusing and full of smoke and mirrors. We believe in giving authors real insight, fair terms, and a clear path forward because trust is the foundation of every great story.

Innovation with Purpose

Were building the future of publishing. Our technology isnt just smart, its human-centered. We use AI to empower authors, not replace them. Innovation means rethinking whats possible so more voices can be heard.

The Author Comes First

Were in this with you. Spines isnt just a platform. Were a partner who believes in your book and helps you make it the best it can be.
